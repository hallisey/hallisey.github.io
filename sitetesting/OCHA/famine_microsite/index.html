The Four Worst Food Crises in the World

More than 20 million people in Nigeria, Somalia, South Sudan and Yemen are experiencing famine or the risk of famine over the coming six months. UN agencies and their humanitarian partners can scale up our response to avert a mass-scale crisis, but we need the money and access to people to do so.
